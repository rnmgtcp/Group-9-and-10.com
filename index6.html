ABIGAIL C. SANTOS

A Journey of Purpose and Resilience

At 22 years old, I am Abigail C. Santos—an ambivert, gracefully balancing the quiet introspection of an introvert with the social spark of an extrovert. My chill demeanor carries me through life’s twists and turns, even when I feel a bit lost. My gaze is fixed on the horizon—I envision myself as a teacher in the future. Why? Because I want to inspire the coming generations. The world’s influence can be overwhelming, and I’ve seen it take its toll. But I don’t aim to be an intimidating intellect; instead, I’ll be the teacher who listens, understands, and cares. My mission is to guide young minds toward growth. Wealth isn’t my goal in this profession. The tough moments, self-doubt, and heartbreaks are part of the journey. Beyond teaching, I harbor other dreams, but it’s never too late to pursue them. Comments from others? I’m learning to weather them—after all, it’s normal in this vast world. This course has been my crucible of self-discovery. I’ve learned, grown, and yearn for more. In the near future, I see myself not just as successful, but as someone who leaves a positive mark on the lives of others.
